Students of classes V to XII are involved in this programme. This is not just about academic instruction; it is about fostering curiosity in students,  

  - Helping to grasp concepts through practical in-hand experiments and everyday observations.
  - Developing Computer skills and coding basics. 
  - Encouraging overall development through scientific enquiry.
  - Informing about career opportunities.
  - Awareness of social responsibilities.
